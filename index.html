Drumsticks Fried

Recipe by Chef Fazila Khan

Ingredients

For Marination

  • Drumsticks 4-6
  • Salt to taste
  • Red chilli powder ½ tsp
  • Cumin powder¼ tsp
  • Garam masala powder1/8 tsp
  • Yogurt ¼ cup

For Coating

  • All purpose flour½ cup
  • Eggs 2 beaten
  • Bread crumbs as required
  • Oil for fry

Cooking Directions

  1. Put all marination ingredients in a bowl and coat on chicken and leave for 2 hours.
  2. Now cook on medium slow heat for 12 minutes. Let it cool.
  3. Heat oil in a wok. Coat chicken piece in all purpose flour then dip in egg and coat in bread crumbs.
  4. Fry in warm oil until golden and crispy.
